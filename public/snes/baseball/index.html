Baseball

Baseball is a game where the player can pitch, hit, field, and run just like in a real baseball game. The player can choose between USA and Japan modes, two different teams, and among a line-up of unique players. In the 1-player game, the player is against the computer, and the in the 2-player game, their friend is their opponent.

Developed by [Nintendo](/search?q=Nintendo?&max-results=12) Published by [Nintendo](/search?q=Nintendo?&max-results=12) Genre: [Sports](/search?q=Sports?&max-results=12) Platform: [Famicom Disk System](/search/label/%40famicom?&max-results=12) Release Date: February 21, 1986 YouTube: [Baseball Gameplay](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=G8DzhmEle5A)
